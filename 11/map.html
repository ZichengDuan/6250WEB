Introduction

By searching for a specific event, Interactive News Map will automatically collect relevant information and intelligently integrate it with the country or region to display it on the world map. This tool will help users explore the connections between different countries and regions on the same event from a macro perspective.

Basic Function

Get you familiar

On the left side of the website, there are three tools help focus on the event, country and data range. Users can enter keywords and country of the event in the ‘News’ and ‘Country’ search engines, also change the time range of the news searched if needed. After that, there will be red dots and red lines in the map. Red dots indicate the countries associated with the event, and lines show the relationship between countries. By moving the cursor over a particular country, relevant news is displayed in the bubble and can be clicked to see more information.

Example Usage

As for ‘China's oil import’ event, we can search "China's oil import" in the search engine, then countries associated with this event will be marked. The more relevant, the more obvious the marking will be. As shown in the figure, we can see that the major importing countries of China's oil include Saudi Arabia, Angola, Russian Federation, Iran, Oman and so on. When we move the cursor to a certain country, we will see the news related to China's oil import, as well as the latest data. For more details, users can click to news link to jump to the information page. .
