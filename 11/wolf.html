Introduction

Model United Nations Conference is an open strategy game. This game provides a platform for players to communicate and discuss some major international events. And it can be a useful tool to help people better understand these events.

Game Rules

Preparations before the game starts

In this game, each player can act as representatives of a country. Before players enter one game, players are required to choose one of some international events as their interested topic. Then the players who choose the same international event will be divided into one game. One game will contain 9-12 players. Once every player is ready, the game starts. When the game starts, each player needs to choose the country which they are interested in.


Game Procedure

This game contains three rounds and each round includes 5 steps. Firstly, the system will provide different information to each player depends on the country they have chosen and all the information are related to the chosen topic. Please notice that information provided in each round will be different. This information is from shallow to deep as the game progresses. In second step, each players will be given 3 minutes to quickly identify their country’s situation and formulate strategies. Thirdly, each player can summarize the information they got and share this information and their strategies with other players. Game system will assign 3 minutes to this step. Then, after each player finish their sharing, all players can have 2 minutes to talk their ideas about other plays’ strategies. After this process, each player has three votes to vote for players whose strategies are better. (Each round, each player has three votes.) After three rounds, the top-3 players who owns the most votes will win the game.

Game Example

1. Choose COVID-19 as your interested topic
2. Choose Australia as your interested country and you will be the representative of Australia.
3. First round starts, you will be given some latest news about COVID-19 situation of Australia.
4. You will be given 3 minutes to read and summarize these news and prepare strategies.
5. You will be given 3 minutes to talk your summarized information and your corresponding strategies which may be useful to better control the COVID-19 situation of Australia. (please notice you will also listen to all others players’ information and strategies)
6. After all each players finishing their statements, you will be given 2 minute to talk several strategies you interested from all these players statement.
7. You have three votes and you can vote for any player with 1-3 votes
8. Second round and third round, same process with 3,4,5,6,7
9. The top-3 players who owns the most votes will win the game.
